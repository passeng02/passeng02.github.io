About

Saimadhav is a masters student in the Industrial Engineering and Operations Research program at IIT Bombay. He previously obtained his bachelor's in Computer Science Engineering from Madras Institute of Technology, Chennai. He is interested in Digital Twin Simulation, Supply Chain, and Artificial Intelligence applications to supply chains. He is working on developing a Digital Twin for a warehouse.

Projects

Optimal Overbooking Model for Airlines
Modelling the overbooking scenario as an optimization model with regularization and solving it computationally.
XAI-Cardiocare : eXplainable AI driven heart valve disease detection
A web based deep learning system that classifies heart sound signals as NORMAL or MVP or MR or MS or AS (Disease classes). My contribution to the project was in training a 2-D CNN model and adding explainability to the system. Libraries used : Tensorflow, Keras, SciPy, NumPy, Flask.
Natal.ml : Perinatal health risk prediction using machine learning
This project involved developing an ML-based web app to predict perinatal health risk based on various vitals of the patient. My contribution to the project involved data cleaning, exploratory data analysis and selection of most suited machine learning model.
Gymkhana - Social network of fitness enthusiasts
Problem Statement : Absence of community or friends to work out along for various fitness activities reduces our efficiency both physically and mentally. There are very few readily available means for finding people exclusively for such situations. My work in the project was backend development.
